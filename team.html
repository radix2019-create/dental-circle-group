Meet The Team

Lead Doctor

Dr. Payam Sooferi

Co-Founder

Payam Sooferi is the co-founder of The Dental Circle. He’s a dentist highly trained in endodontics (root canals) and surgery. Dr. Sooferi graduated from the University of California Los Angeles (UCLA) with a Bachelor of Science degree in Biology.

He went on to earn his Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) from the prestigious University of Southern California with Dean’s List honors. Dr. Sooferi is committed to delivering the highest quality care to his patients.

In addition to his formal education, he continuously furthers his knowledge of dentistry by attending educational seminars and study groups. His experience, caring personality, joyful spirit, and dedication, as well as his in-depth understanding of today’s technology, allow him to offer the latest dental care to his patients while recognizing that treatment should be affordable.
